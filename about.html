Our Story

Built by Engineers, for Engineers

Corelon Systems was founded in 2018 by a group of infrastructure and software engineers who saw a gap: too many IT consultancies offered strategy without hands-on execution.

Founded in 2018 and headquartered in Uganda, Corelon Systems is a technology partner that architects solutions and builds them, operates them, and stands behind them 24/7. With coordination personnel in England, we serve enterprises, scaleups, and public institutions worldwide.

Our Journey

Company Timeline

2018

Founded in Uganda

Three infrastructure engineers launch Corelon Systems in Entebbe, Uganda, with a focus on cloud migration and server management.

2019

Development Division

Expanded into custom software development, adding backend, mobile, and web capabilities.

2020

First Enterprise Client

Signed FinCorp as our first large-scale enterprise client. Delivered full cloud migration in 8 weeks.

2021

England Coordination Office

Established coordination personnel in England to serve European markets. Team grew to 25 members.

2022

Data & Analytics Practice

Launched our data science and analytics division, adding BI, ML, and data engineering to our portfolio.

2023

ISO 27001 Certified

Achieved ISO 27001 certification. Expanded government and healthcare sector work.

2024

100th Project Milestone

Delivered our 100th project. Client retention rate hit 98%. Team expanded to 40+ engineers.

2026

Today

Serving 60+ organisations worldwide from our Uganda headquarters with nine specialised service domains and 24/7 support.
